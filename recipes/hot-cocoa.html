Hot Cocoa

Description

There is nothing quite like hot cocoa in the winter. So, bundle up and get ready to make this soul-warming holiday goodie!

P.S. Don't hesitate to make this recipe your own - garnish this classic drink with your favorite sweet treats!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup cocoa
  • Dash salt
  • 1/3 cup hot water
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, combine the sugar, cocoa and salt. Add the water, and bring to a boil. Cook and stir for two minutes.
  2. Stir in the milk. Heat to serving temperature (do not boil). Remove from the heat, and stir in the vanilla. Whisk until frothy.
  3. If desired, garnish each serving with marshmallows, whipped cream or cinnamon sticks.
